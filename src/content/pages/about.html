Who We Are

KMS Plumbing & Gasfitting is a trusted local company in Edmonton, Alberta, proudly owned and operated by Jack Kumar, a Red Seal-certified plumber and gasfitter with over 15 years of industry experience. We specialize in safe, reliable, and efficient plumbing and gasfitting services designed to protect your home and family.

Our Commitment to Safety
We exceed industry standards by:

  • Using high-quality materials and secure licensed subcontractors
  • Conducting hazard assessments to identify and mitigate potential risks (e.g., asbestos, electrical hazards)
  • Adhering to the latest safety protocols, including the National Plumbing Code (NPC 2019) and WHMIS regulations
  • Utilizing PPE and maintaining safe work practices on every job
