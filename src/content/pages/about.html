About Us

Who We Are

Founded in 2012, KMS Plumbing & Gasfitting Ltd is an owner-operated provider of plumbing, drain cleaning, and gasfitting services in the Edmonton area. We strive to provide exceptional customer service and quality workmanship at a fair price. We are dedicated to providing a seamless and hassle-free service experience with prompt communication, professional service, and upfront pricing.
